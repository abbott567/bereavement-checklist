Do you need help to pay for the funeral?

You may be able to get a Funeral Payment if you are struggling to pay for a funeral you are arranging.

How much you could get depends on your circumstances and whether or not you qualify. Get more information on Funeral Payments.

For more advice on help with paying for funerals, visit:

  • the Money Advice Service page on paying for a funeral
  • the Citizens Advice page on what to do after a death
  • the Turn2us page on applying for a Budgeting Loan applying for a Budgeting Loan
