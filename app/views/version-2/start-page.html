Let the DWP know that someone has died

When someone dies, any State Pension or benefits they recieved will need to be cancelled.

You'll need to give the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) some details about the person who has died. This should only take a few minutes and will help you understand what needs to be done in the days and weeks following their death.

The DWP will also check if you can get help with funeral costs or other benefits.

You don't have to do this yourself. You can ask a trusted friend or family member to act on your behalf.
