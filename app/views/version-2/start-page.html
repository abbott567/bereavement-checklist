Tell us someone has died

When someone dies, any State Pension, allowances or benefits they received will need to be cancelled.

You'll need to give some details about the person who has died. This should only take a few minutes and will let government organisations see what needs to be stopped or updated.

You can also get information on help with funeral costs or other benefits.

You don't have to do this yourself. You can ask a trusted friend or family member to act on your behalf.
