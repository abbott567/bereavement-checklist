Before you start

To be eligible for Bereavement Support Payment

You must:

  • be under State Pension age
  • Have been married to or in a civil partnership with your spouse when they died
  • have been living in the UK when your spouse died
  • make a claim no more than a year after your spouse’s death

You’ll need the following to complete your application

  • your National Insurance number
  • your late spouse’s National Insurance number
  • the date your spouse died
  • your bank details
