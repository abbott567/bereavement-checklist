Before you start

To get Bereavement Support Payment you must:

  • be under State Pension age
  • have been married or in a civil partnership when your spouse or civil partner died
  • have been living in the UK when your spouse or civil partner died
  • make a claim within a year of your spouse or civil partner’s death

To make your application you’ll need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • your spouse or civil partner’s National Insurance number
  • the date your spouse or civil partner died
  • your bank details

You'll also be asked whether you have any dependent children. If you do, you could get a higher rate of Bereavement Support Payment.
