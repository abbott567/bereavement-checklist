Have you applied for Child Benefit?

If you're entitled to Child Benefit, the amount of Bereavement Support Payment you'll get may be affected.

You can choose not to get Child Benefit payments, but you should still apply because:

  • it will help you get National Insurance credits which count towards your State Pension
  • it will ensure your child is registered to get a National Insurance number when they are 16 years old
