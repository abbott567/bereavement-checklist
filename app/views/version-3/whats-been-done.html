What's being done

Thank you for telling us about the death of {{deceasedName}} at what must be a very difficult time. The following organisations have been told:

Department for Work and Pensions (DWP)
  • Incapacity Benefit - this will be stopped
  • Income Support - this will be stopped
  • Jobseeker's Allowance - this will be stopped
  • Universal Credit - this will still be paid to the partner of {{deceasedName}} but the amount will change
HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC)
  • Working Tax Credits - this will be stopped
  • Child Tax Credits - this will still be paid to the partner of {{deceasedName}} but the amount will change
  • Child Benefit - this will still be paid to the partner of {{deceasedName}} but the amount will change
Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency (DVLA)
  • Driving licence - this will be cancelled to prevent it being used fraudulently
HM Passport Office
  • UK passport - this will be cancelled to prevent it being used fraudulently
Your local authority
  • Council tax - North Tyneside Council has been notified

If any more information is needed about {{deceasedName}} by any of the organisations listed, they will contact you - you don't need to do anything else right now.
